Assignment 1

This is a static mobile website to plan and describe an app called Hillbaggers.

It is required to have a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 20 screens. Included are 6 screens.

It includes initial planning designs for the app under the following 5 headings:

  4. Critique of similar apps
  5. Technologies to be used in the app
